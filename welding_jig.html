Welding Jig

Designed and built a welding jig for the UCI Frame Building club

Background

In order to weld a bicycle frame together it is necessary to fixture the tubes in a consistent and calculated fashion. Commercially available welding jigs were outside of our clubs budget so designing our own was the only option. A good welding jig is easy to set and relatively accurate (compromising on dimensional accuracy was a major theme throughout the design process). Our first iteration
